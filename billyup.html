Experiences

This game was made as a challenge for me which is to complete a simple game in few days in Unity, in this project I improve my skill using gravity and force.

Information

"Billy Up" is an engaging and challenging endless jumping game that tests players' skills and reflexes. The objective of the game is to accumulate points by surviving as long as possible, reaching each floor without running out of jumps. Players are given three jump attempts per jump, and if they fail to reach the next floor, they must fall back to the previous floor to recover their jump attempts. The game's simple yet addictive gameplay mechanics make it easy for players of all ages to enjoy. The graphics are colorful and engaging, adding to the game's overall appeal. As players progress through the game, the difficulty level increases, providing a more challenging experience that keeps players coming back for more. Overall, "Billy Up" is a fun and entertaining game that provides hours of endless jumping fun.
