Experiences

I completed a challenging project in which I created a simple VR game in just a few days using Unity. Through this experience, I gained valuable knowledge and skills related to setting up a VR game, including how to properly configure the necessary settings and implement VR-specific functionality. Despite the tight deadline, I was able to successfully complete the project, which has bolstered my confidence in my ability to work efficiently under pressure and within tight timelines. This experience has inspired me to continue expanding my expertise in VR game development and explore more complex projects in the future.

Information

It is a virtual reality (VR) bowling game that utilizes the immersive experience of VR to create a fun and engaging gameplay experience. Using Unity, I designed the game to simulate the experience of being in a real-life bowling alley, complete with realistic physics and a user-friendly interface. This project allowed me to hone my VR development skills, as well as gain experience in game design, programming, and user experience. By completing this project, I feel more confident in my ability to create engaging VR games and am excited to continue exploring the possibilities of this technology.
