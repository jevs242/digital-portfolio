As part of my final project for my HTML class at Atlantic University College, I designed and created a simple website aimed at informing the gaming community about the latest news and updates in the video game industry. The website provides helpful tips and guides to assist gamers in achieving all the trophies for their favorite video games. The site is user-friendly and easy to navigate, making it a valuable resource for avid gamers seeking to stay informed and enhance their gaming experience. This project allowed me to apply my HTML skills in a practical and engaging way, while contributing to the gaming community's knowledge base.
