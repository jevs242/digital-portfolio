Kon Monster

Information

Kon Monster is an exciting platform side-scroller game where players must fight their way through various obstacles in order to reach the end of the level. In this game, players can either break objects or avoid them as they make their way through each stage. The project was developed using C++ programming language and was created as part of the "Advance Programming of Videogames" course.
