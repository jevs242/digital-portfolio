Experiences

This project was created for a gamejam with the theme of healing. I decided to create a 2D game set in the water because I had never made a 2D game in Unreal Engine before. Throughout the project, I learned how to use sprites in Unreal Engine and gained experience in creating a 2D game. The game is available on itch.io.

Information

In this game, you play as a shark and your goal is to survive the water pollution caused by an oil accident. You have to navigate the ocean and eat fish to heal, but you must be careful of the larger sharks that can kill you. Additionally, you need to avoid garbage that can harm your health and the pollution that gradually takes away your health. It's a challenging survival game with an important message about the effects of pollution on ocean life.
