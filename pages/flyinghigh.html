Flying High

Experiences

In this game, I learned the process of game development during a game jam with an interdimensional theme. Drawing inspiration from this theme, I conceived an idea featuring a hippie who embarks on a journey to another dimension with the aid of his trusty bong.

Information

"Flying High" is an entertaining clicker game that challenges players to help Bob, the game's protagonist, enter another dimension through his bong by clicking on him. The game features colorful and engaging graphics that draw players in. Overall, "Flying High" is a fun and short game.
