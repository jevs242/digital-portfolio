Card Game : Briscas

Experiences

For my first major project, I decided to create a digital version of the popular card game "Briscas" for a class assignment. Despite the challenge of completing a large project within a tight deadline, I persevered and successfully created a functional and engaging game. Throughout the process, I learned valuable lessons about project management, time management, and programming techniques. Looking back on the project, I now see areas where I could have improved my use of functions, parameters, and class structures. However, I am proud of what I accomplished and happy to have gained valuable experience in game development.

Information

The project is about a type of text game which is a ‘Briscas with Spanish cards’ game. The project was made in the ‘Advanced Programming’.
